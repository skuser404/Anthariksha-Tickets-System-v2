# Member Guide

## Signing in
Use the email + password your administrator created for you. Tick **Remember me**
to stay signed in. You can enable **email two-factor** and **login alerts** under
**Profile → Security**. Sessions end automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity.

## Submitting a ticket
1. Book the permit on the official **Aranya Vihara** website first.
2. In the portal, open **Add Ticket**.
3. Enter the official **Ticket ID**, **booking email**, **booking date**, **trek
   date**, choose the **trek**, and the **number of persons**.
4. The **permit price** auto-loads and your **commission** (₹50 × persons) is shown live.
5. Submit — you'll see a confetti confirmation and the ticket enters **Pending Verification**.

> Each Ticket ID can only be submitted once. The system also flags unusual
> entries (e.g. a booking email reused elsewhere) for the admin to review.

## Tracking your work
- **Dashboard** — your totals, balance, monthly earnings and personalised insights.
- **My Tickets** — every submission with its status; click for the lifecycle timeline.
- **Payment History (My Earnings)** — commission earned, total paid, current balance and receipts.
- **Refund History** — any cancellations/refunds tied to your tickets.
- **Notifications** — approvals, rejections, payments and refund progress.

## Ticket statuses
`Pending Verification → Approved` (commission credited) **or** `Not Confirmed`
(reason shown). Approved tickets that are later cancelled go
`Cancelled → Refund Pending → Refund Completed`. Records are never deleted.

## Getting paid
Commission is credited when an admin approves your ticket. Admins record payouts
against your balance — track them under **My Earnings**. Balance = earned − paid.

Questions? See **Help & Support** or contact an administrator.
